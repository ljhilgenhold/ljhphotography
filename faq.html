Newborn Session FAQ
Q: When should I schedule my newborn's session?

A: You should schedule your newborn photoshoot around your baby's due date. Once your baby arrives, we can solidify a date and time. A newborn session must take place between the first 7 - 20 days of your baby's birth.

Q: What do I need to do before my newborn's session?

A: It is ideal that baby be fed right before the session so they are more likely to sleep through the photoshoot. You should also have any outfits, blankets, or accessories ready for the session.

Q: What if my baby is born early?

A: If your baby arrives before your due date, we can try to make arrangements to schedule your photoshoot earlier. If your baby is in the NICU, we will wait until your baby is discharged from the hospital.

Q: How many photos can I expect?

A: You can expect between 10-15 photos for your newborn session.

Couples & Engagements FAQ
Q: How do engagement sessions work?

A: For engagement sessions, I do both candid engagement shoots, where I photograph your proposal, as well as planned engagement annoncement photoshoots.

Q: Can you also photograph our wedding?

A: Unfortunately, I do not offer any day-of wedding photography. However, if you would like to schedule bridal portraits or bride and groom portraits before or after your wedding, we can make arrangements! To schedule this, just select "couples/engagements" on the inquiry form and enter "bridal portraits" or "bride and groom portraits" in the comments box!

Family Portraits FAQ
Q: How many family members can be in the family portaits?

A: For family portraits, please keep it limited to immediate family only. (ex: mom, dad, & kids)

Q: Can pets be included in the family portaits?

A: Of course! Well-behaved pets are always welcome to be a part of the family portraits! Just let me know if you would like to include pets in your session inquiry so that locations that accomodate pets can be considered in planning your photoshoot!
